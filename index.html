About NCEF

Supporting church plants for over 60 years.

The New Church Evangelism Fellowship (NCEF) is a mission-driven organization dedicated to planting and strengthening new Christian churches. Since 1961, we have supported over 45 church plants across the Midwest by providing financial assistance, mentorship, and prayer. Our goal is to establish sustainable, self-sufficient congregations that share the Gospel and impact their communities.

What We Do

NCEF provides structured support to new churches through financial aid, mentorship, and strategic growth planning.

  • Provide tiered financial support for five years.
  • Offer mentorship and prayer support for church leaders.
  • Help churches build strong, self-sustaining communities.
  • Ensure 100% of donations go directly to church plants.

Success Stories

Our supported churches are thriving and making a difference in their communities.

Restore Christian Church

Located in Highland, IL, Restore Christian Church has launched a Care Ministry to serve families in need, expanding its outreach through acts of service and faith.

Engage Christian Church

Thriving in suburban Chicago, Engage Christian Church successfully navigated challenges like pandemic disruptions and has become a self-sustaining ministry.
